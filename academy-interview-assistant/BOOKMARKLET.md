# Academy Test Panel - Bookmarklet

## Option 1: Test Button (Paste Once in Console)

1. Open Google Meet
2. Press F12 to open DevTools Console
3. Copy and paste the contents of `add-test-button.js`
4. Press Enter
5. You'll see a "ðŸŽ¯ Test Panel" button in the bottom right
6. Click it to toggle the candidate panel on/off

## What the Test Shows

The test panel displays mock data for "Jane Smith" with:
- Name, position, and stage
- LinkedIn, Portfolio, and GitHub links styled in your Academy colors
- Draggable panel
- All hover effects and animations
- Olive-green Academy branding

This lets you see exactly how the panel will look when a real candidate is detected!
